About us

Entrepreneurship Development Cell was established at R.V.College of Engineering, Mysore Road, Bangalore -560 059 in April 2007 under the sponsorship of NSTEDB Division, Dept. of Science and Technology, Government of India for promoting Entrepreneurship awareness among Engineering & Technology students, Science & Technology students, Diploma Students and Management students and to make them to choose Entrepreneurship as one of their Career option.

Entrepreneurship Development Cell at RVCE will strive hard to promote technology application and become a resource base in identifying emerging and innovative Entrepreneurial opportunities.

E-SUMMIT, 2018

The Entrepreneurship Cell was formed by students and some Professors of IIT Bombay with the mission of promoting the culture of entrepreneurship within the campus of IIT Bombay in October, 1998. Since then the Cell has launched several initiatives for budding entrepreneurs in IIT Bombay and other colleges across India. E-Cell, IIT Bombay was one of the founding members of the National Entrepreneurship Network

E-Summit will be a three-day event at RV College of Engineering to spread awareness about entrepreneurship and help the aspiring entrepreneurs to take the next step towards their dream. We are also aiming at bringing all the startups from RVCE (including alumni) under a single umbrella.

E-Summit will be a platform for all like-minded people to interact, learn about entrepreneurship. The best from the industry will be here to inspire and encourage the next generation of entrepreneurs.
